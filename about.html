I made this site as an experiment. I'm curious about the future and about how different things fit together, from markets and technology to nature and the environment.

I studied environmental economics and management, and I tend to explore ideas through research, deep dives, and tools. Sometimes that takes me into crypto and markets, sometimes into sustainability and ecology, and sometimes into building things with AI.

I've also spent time with nature, freediving, breathwork, and travel, and those experiences shape how I pay attention and make sense of things.

I don't know exactly what this site will become. For now I'm publishing essays and projects instead of keeping everything in my folders. It will evolve as I do.

You can just do things.
